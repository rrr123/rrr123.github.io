46N Recycled Mechanical Elastic Yarn

Nan Ya Vietnam Fiber Division

Product Overview

"46N is a uniquely engineered recycled polyester elastic yarn. Utilizing high-quality rPET as the base material combined with S & Z twist technology, we provide solutions that balance stable physical properties with high elasticity. Designed for consistency and processability in both performance and fashion markets."

Core Advantages

Sustainability

100% rPET material, ESG compliant, GRS & OEKO-TEX 100 certified.

Technical Stability

S&Z twist tech ensures high strength, low breakage, and superior yield.

Aesthetics

Cotton-Like touch with excellent dye uniformity and smooth fabric surface.

Core Specs & Customization

  • 65/72 rPET FD + 50/36 46N (S&Z)
  • 30/48 rCD + 50/36 46N (S&Z)
  • 70/72 rPET FD + 50/36 46N Cotton-Like
  • Options: Semi-Dull / Full-Dull / Bright / Cooling / Anti-UV
